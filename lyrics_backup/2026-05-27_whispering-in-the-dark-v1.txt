[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Oh, the shadows fall, but the fire is wide awake
I can feel the pull in every single breath you take
There’s a quiet storm, and we’re standing in the spark
Just you and me, whispering in the dark

[Verse 1]
The porch light flickers down the gravel road
But in this room, we've got nowhere to go
You trace the wood grain on the table top
I’m hoping that this midnight doesn't stop

[Pre-Chorus]
The clock is ticking, but the time is standing still
A quiet hunger that I know we’re gonna fill
You look at me with that secret in your eyes
The kind of heat that takes me by surprise

[Chorus]
Oh, we’re burning like a lantern in the wind
Where the memory ends, the night begins
I pull you closer, let the shadows leave their mark
Falling into you, whispering in the dark

[Post-Chorus]
[Echo] (Whispering in the dark)
Oh, let the world fade out tonight

[Verse 2]
Your fingertips are painting along my skin
A slow-burn rhythm and I’m drawn right in
The amber glow is catching on your face
And every word you breathe is my saving grace

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Echo] (Whispering in the dark)
Oh, let the world fade out tonight

[Bridge]
The embers are fading, but the feeling is alive
I’m giving you everything I have to survive
Just you and Saeed, getting lost inside the flame
No going back to the way we were before you came

[Spoken]
